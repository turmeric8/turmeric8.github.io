ABOUT OUR TURMERIC

Turmeric8 is a specially grown and processed turmeric with the world’s highest level of curcumin at 8%. Dried using a proprietary low temperature process, it is the closest to raw turmeric of any turmeric powder.

IT ALL STARTS WITH THE SOIL

Turmeric8 is harvested in the organic soils of Gujarat, India. Unlike conventional farming, our soil is cultivated with our specialised preperation of amrut soil, a soil boasting with essential microbes, enzymes and minerals.

This rich soil is necessary to increase the plants phytosynthesis and general nutrient profile. As you know, rich soils always equal rich produce.

I believe whole turmeric is more effective than isolated curcumin for inflammatory disorders. - Andrew Weil, M.D.

COLD DRIED FOR MAXIMUM NUTRIENT RETENTION

Turmeric8 is dried under cold temperatures in a proprietary process which protects the heat and oxygen sensitive bioactive constituents of fresh turmeric.

We also utilize special cold-milling equipment which grinds the freshly dried turmeric to a fine powder. The blades of the mill are cooled to ensure non of the bioactive constituents preserved in the drying process are lost.

These processes, along with our unique growing conditions guarantee the world’s most potent turmeric powder, with 8% curcumin.

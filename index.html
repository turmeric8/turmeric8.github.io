About Us

We are passionate about sourcing. Present us with an idea, an ingredient, and we become fixated. We scour the internet to the deepest depths. We call, email and message. We call it making noise. If we can’t find it, It will find us. Biobotanics was founded as a retail business, selling specialty extracts. After many years in business, we had an epiphany.

After developing many relationships within the nutraceutical, complementary medicine industry, we found ourselves sourcing ingredients for our contacts. Call it, destiny. Life sometimes leads you in a direction. It’s up to you to listen, and we did. We love what we do, and when it comes to sourcing, you cannot count on anyone else. Our passion to source will drive us further than any competitor, anywhere.

Put your trust in us, for guidance, quality and most importantly, to understand your passion. As business owners, we understand the good fight. We love helping, so let us do what we do and we will help you drive your passion to succeed.
